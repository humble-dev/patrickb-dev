Andromeda

Our Intergalactic Neighbor

M31 was discovered by Edward Hubble in the 20's and it is the nearest galaxy
to our own Milky Way. It is about twice the size of our galaxy. In about 5 billion
years, the two galaxies will collide with one another and, though a bunch of stars
will be ejected from both galaxies, the rest will be incorporated into a huge super-galaxy.
Which is crazy.
