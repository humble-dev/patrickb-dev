Polaris

The North Star

As the earth spins, the north star pretty much stays in place. Due to procession
however (the world being tilted on its axis), it north won't always point to Polaris.
In a few thousand years it will probably point to a star in Draco. But for now, we are
grateful to have the north star to light and guide the way.
On clear nights at least.
