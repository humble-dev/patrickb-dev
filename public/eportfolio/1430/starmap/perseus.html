Perseus

Holding the Severed Head of Medusa

Snake-haired Medusa had been turning everyone who had met her gaze into stone.
So the king enlisted Perseus who, by using a shield so polished it was like a mirror,
found Medusa and smote her head, which is where baby Pegasus sprang out of.

Its brightest star is called Algol which means demon's head in Arabic.
It is one of the eyes of Medusa's head.
