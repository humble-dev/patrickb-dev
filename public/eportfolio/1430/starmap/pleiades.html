Pleiades

The Seven Sisters

I heard once that if you were conscripted to be a soldier in Ancient Greece,
they would ask you to count the stars you could see in the Pleiades.
Most would say 6 or 7, but if you counted 8, you were chosen to be a scout.

Another story is that Orion chased after the seven beautiful sisters for hundreds of years
until Zeus took pity on them and turned them into stars. Of course, Orion was turned into
stars too and continues to chase them across the sky.
